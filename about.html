About Us

Thesso is a product focussed technology and services provider for solar developers. Thesso’s unique ground screw based Mounting structures enable developers to roll out solar capacity faster and cheaper.

Thesso is co-founded by V. Venugopal and T. Sushanth. The founders and the rest of the team at Thesso have a combined experience of commissioning Grid connected ground mounted solar parks to the tune of 85 MW across various states in India. The team has deep relationships with solar developers, having offered EPC solutions.

The founders intend to leverage their depth of solar EPC experience to meet the exacting demands of project developers on time and cost per MW.

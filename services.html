Product and Services

Our solution for concrete-less foundations of solar PV mounting structures involving ground screws replaces the conventional solution. This is much faster and removes the need for enforcing elaborate quality monitoring mechanisms to ensure quality of the concrete mix , does away with curing time and reduces the foundation readiness time from 20-24 days to just around 8-11 days per MW installed capacity.

We install the mounting structures, fix the panels, do the DC cabling and hand over the DC field array – on a end –to – end basis. We offer following services which are part of developing a solar unit.

  • Land survey and contour mapping
  • Engineering and designing of DC field
  • Detailing and Fabrication of mounting structures
  • Ground screw based installations
  • Civil work for Mounting structure foundations where needed
  • Panel erection
  • DC Cable trenching and routing including data cables
  • Cable terminations
  • Commissioning of DC Field
